# Modding Policy

his project supports and encourages modding.

- Mods may be free or paid.
- Mod authors are fully responsible for their content.
- The project maintainers are not responsible for third-party mods.

Mods must not:

- Use the project trademark, logo, or branding.
- Claim to be official or endorsed builds.

## Mod Contributions to the Core Game

Mods and third-party content are NOT considered Contributions under the Apache License unless explicitly submitted as such.

If you wish to have a mod or part of a mod incorporated into the core game, you must:

- Submit it via a pull request to this repository, OR
- Explicitly license the content under a permissive license compatible with Apache License 2.0, OR
- Provide explicit written permission to the project maintainers.

Content not meeting these criteria will not be incorporated.
